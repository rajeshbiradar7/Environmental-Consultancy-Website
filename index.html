Our Consulting Services

Our consulting services cover all environment-related requirements for an ongoing concern or an upcoming facility. Depending on the purpose and processes planned, SEC will provide technical assistance to successfully fulfill all regulatory requirements.

  • • Environmental Studies, Research and Assessment
  • • Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA)
  • • Detailed Project Report (DPR)
  • • Risk Management Studies
  • • Waste Management Studies
  • • Cost-Benefit Analysis
  • • Pre-Feasibility Reports
  • • Social Impact Assessment
  • • Due Diligence
  • • Regulatory Compliance
  • • Strategic Analysis
  • • Environmental Retainership
  • • Policy Analysis
  • • Engineering Compliance
  • • Environmental Management Plan
  • • Scope of Work / Terms of Reference (SOW/TOR)
